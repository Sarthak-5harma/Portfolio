Doctor Appointment

  • Our team created a website to book doctor appointments with doctor of your choice online.

    You can book token online at the time you want and can see the availability of a doctor at particular hospital at particular time.

Respiratory Disorder Detection

  • Our team created a project which is used for detecting and locating diseases from chest x-ray.

    You can upload an x-ray of your chest and the AI based CADx software will detect the disease for you can provide you with heat map to show location of the diseased area.

    We created this project during an event NEXT hackthon.


    This project was the winner of NEXT(NCIT EXPO ON TECHNOLOGY) hackthon 2019.
